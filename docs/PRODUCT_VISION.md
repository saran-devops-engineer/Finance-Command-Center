# Product Vision

Finance Command Center is a financial decision support system for individuals in India managing multiple commitments.

It should help users understand their financial life in less than two minutes and confidently decide what to do next.

## Not An Expense Tracker

The product should not optimize for categorizing every rupee.

It should optimize for:

- financial clarity
- stress reduction
- decision confidence
- debt awareness
- upcoming obligation visibility
- safe use of available money

## Target Users

Initial users are individuals in India who currently manage finances using Excel, notes, bank apps, and memory.

Common commitments:

- Home loans
- Gold loans
- Personal loans
- Vehicle loans
- Education loans
- Credit card EMIs
- E-commerce EMIs
- Hand loans
- Insurance
- Rent
- Utility bills
- Salary and income
- Fixed expenses

## Core Promise

When users open the app, they should feel:

> I finally know what is happening with my finances.

Then immediately:

> I know exactly what to do next.

## Product Questions

Every major screen should answer one question:

- Home: Am I financially okay today?
- Loans: Which loan needs attention?
- Money: How much money can I safely use?
- Insights: What should I do next?
- Profile: How is my data managed?

## V1 Product Strategy

V1 validates the product vision, navigation, information architecture, local-first privacy, and core financial decision flow.

Prefer simplicity over feature completeness.

Any feature that does not help the user make a better financial decision should be postponed.
