# Money Prompt

Use this prompt when improving Money.

## Screen Question

How much money can I safely use?

## Goals

- Explain income versus commitments.
- Make available money easy to understand.
- Separate mandatory and flexible spending.
- Support editing monthly cash-flow assumptions.

## Rules

- Do not become a full expense tracker in V1.
- Keep the summary simple.
- Preserve local-first data.
- Use plain financial language.

## Good Improvements

- Category summaries.
- Better cash-flow editing.
- Monthly commitment breakdown.
- Emergency buffer guidance.
- Friendly empty states.

## Avoid

- Transaction-heavy UI.
- Complex charts without decisions.
- Shame-based spending language.
